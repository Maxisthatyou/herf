About Cadets

The Royal Canadian Air Cadet is a youth program ran nationally by the Canadian Armed Forces. The program is targeted to spark interest in aviation, leadership, along with other interest areas. To learn more, click below!

The Cadet Program

The Cadet program involves a variety of activities that trains cadets on their skillsets mentioned in About. They are activites such as FTXs, Hanger Tours, Fam Flying, City tours and Sports nights. To learn more about activities within the program, click below!

Ranks and Medals

In the RCAC program, cadets rank up just like in the military. Some of these ranks are given based on service time, or they'll be earned through hard work. As well, they will recieve medals and badges with the completion of certain requirements. TO learn more about this, click on "Learn More".
